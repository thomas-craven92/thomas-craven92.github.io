Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand, manage, and use emotions effectively both for yourself and others.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ is a measure of your own abilities related to problem solving and learning, EQ relates to your emotional and social skills that help in both human and introspective interaction.

Why is EQ important?

Due to the constant changing nature of the world, life and our relationships, it is good to be able to control and use your emotions to your own benefit.
