Te Whare Tapa Wha

Here are some of my wellbeing goals.
Also on the topic of wellbeing, mental health awareness week is coming up.
Here is a good article on Te Whare Tapa Whā from the MHAW 2024 website + ways to get involved and cool things happening around the country!

Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) 1 x Run/Cycle/Pilates/Moto at least 4 times a week
Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) Walk the dog with my partner daily, cook for one another, check in on my family often.
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Take time to stop and be, with no distractions, at least once a day.
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Take study breaks to look out the window and show appreciation to the world (in conjunction with Taha Hinengaro).
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Get out in nature, be it forest, sea or mountain, stop and take it all in at least 3 times a week.
